Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SmartPPE implementation and features

How long does the implementation take?

Typical implementation takes 2-4 weeks depending on the number of entry points and existing infrastructure. Our team handles the entire setup process, including training your staff and integrating with existing systems.

What happens during the free demo?

During the 30-minute demo, you'll see the system in action, learn about customization options for your site, get a ROI analysis based on your current operations, and have all your questions answered by our safety experts.

Does the system work in harsh mining environments?

Yes, SmartPPE is designed specifically for harsh industrial environments. Our hardware is IP67-rated, operates in extreme temperatures (-20°C to +60°C), and includes dust protection. The system continues working even during network outages.

Can it integrate with our existing systems?

SmartPPE integrates seamlessly with most HRMS, attendance systems, and safety management platforms through our REST API. We provide technical support for custom integrations and data migration.

What kind of support do you provide?

We provide 24/7 technical support, regular system updates, staff training programs, and dedicated account management. Our support team includes mining safety experts who understand your operational challenges.
